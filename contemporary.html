Contemporary Dream Pop

One of the most prominent dream pop bands of the past 20 years is Beach House, formed in 2004. With the release of their first album in 2006, self-titled Beach House, the sound of popular dream pop music shifted from alt-rock inspired to something more mellow. Since then, the band has released 8 full-length studio albums, the most recent of which is titled Once Twice Melody, released in 2022.
